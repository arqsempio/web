# Torres Ministerios (Justice & Production)
**Institutional Government Headquarters | 14,700 m²**

![Torres Ministerios Photo](images/torres-ministerios.jpg)

### Project Overview
A vertical urban complex housing the Ministries of Justice and Production. This project required precise coordination due to its institutional nature and high-density office requirements.

### Role: Technical Supervision & Coordination
I led the management and control of the civil works, focusing on technical efficiency and institutional standards.

**Key Contributions:**
* **Strategic Supervision:** Directed the construction management process, ensuring structural integrity and adherence to architectural design.
* **Interdisciplinary Coordination:** Managed the integration of complex building systems (HVAC, fire safety, and fiber optics) across multiple floors.
* **Public Sector Compliance:** Ensured all phases of the project met strict government regulations and public works standards.
* **Resource Optimization:** Applied digital management tools to track progress and optimize material procurement for large-scale institutional use.
